Who we are

Providing and developing Africa’s foremost economic zone for local and international business.

Lekki Worldwide Investments Limited is an investment company, incorporated in 2006, by the Lagos State Government to facilitate the development and management of the Lekki Free Zone; an exclusive economic zone located on 16,500 hectares of land in the Ibeju-Lekki-Epe axis of Lagos State. The Zone was established to maximize the development of infrastructure, industrial capacity, and the tourism potentials of the fastest growing city of West Africa.

The principal objective of the company is to attract new industries & technology and securing economically viable investments that will transform the region into a modern city. The Industrial metropolis is set to boost employment as it will develop into a significant regional hub for various sectors, including manufacturing, trade, real estate, hospitality, leisure facilities, and so on. Ultimately, Lekki Free Zone will eventually transform into one of the largest and most preferred mixed-use investment opportunities and destinations in Nigeria.
