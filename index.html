Why Lekki Free Zone

Providing and developing Africa’s foremost economic zone for local and international business.

Lekki Worldwide Investments Limited is an investment company, incorporated in 2006, by the Lagos State Government to facilitate the development and management of the Lekki Free Zone; an exclusive economic zone located on 16,500 hectares of land in the Ibeju-Lekki-Epe axis of Lagos State.

The Zone was established to maximize the development of infrastructure, industrial capacity, and the tourism potentials of the fastest growing city of West Africa.

Setting up your business in Lekki Free Zone

The focus at LWIL is to create a dynamic environment which guarantees safety of investments vis-à-vis adequate returns on investment, in a socially responsible way beneficial to all stakeholders.
