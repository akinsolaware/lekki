Dabiri, Abiodun

Chairman

Dabiri, Abiodun

Chairman

Biodun Dabiri is a consummate corporate finance specialist who has handled some of the largest and most complex transactions in the Nigerian capital markets over a period spanning 24 years. He was involved in the conceptualization, capitalization, and restructuring of a wide variety of ideas and companies. He set up and nurtured several successful business units, including the Capital Consortium Limited, an asset management company, and Security Swaps Limited, a stockbroking firm and foremost player in the Nigerian capital market.

Biodun, in conjunction with a group of investors, packaged the corporate spin-off of Guaranty Trust Bank Plcâ€™s shareholdings in Magnum Trust Bank Plc (now part of Sterling Bank Plc) and co-managed the bank from 1999 to 2007. Under the Nigerian banking consolidation directives, he put together the investment banking competencies of the banks that merged into Sterling Bank Plc, to form Sterling Capital Markets, which he managed until his resignation in 2008. During this period, Sterling Capital Markets Limited rose to become one of the best- known investment banking institutions in Nigeria. He led a team that raised over N650billion in 25 transactions for the financial services, oil, and manufacturing sectors. He played a prominent role in merger and acquisition activities, local and offshore fundraising activities consummated during the first and second rounds of the financial sector consolidation exercises.

Biodun is a 1977 graduate of the University of Ibadan, a fellow of the Institute of Chartered Accountant of Nigeria having trained with Deloitte, Haskins & Sells (now Deloitte Touche) Chartered Accountants. He is a member of the Board of the Central Securities Clearing System (CSCS) [ the central depository of the Nigerian capital market, which he helped midwife], a fellow of the Chartered Institute of Stockbrokers and a dealing member of the Nigerian Stock Exchange. He is also a committee member of the Lagos Ogun Megacity Development Authority.

Sodade, Tunde

Managing Director

Before joining Lekki Worldwide Investments Limited, Dr. Tunde Sodade served the Ogun State Government where he led the Public Partnership office, Dr. Tunde Sodade was in charge of the Capital Projects Standard and Resource Centre at Regional Municipality of Wood Buffalo- the cradle of the energy exploration and project development in North America.

His responsibilities included the strategic and operational leadership of the center to support the delivery of approximately $1 billion worth of major capital/ infrastructure project including transportation, water, power and pipelines for client residents which included significant oil and gas companies, oil service, heavy engineering, educational and other industrial, commercial and residential stakeholders.

Dr. Sodade earlier consulted for Ernest & Youngâ€™s Major Capital Projects Advisory Services practice in Calgary, Alberta, where he focused on the oil and gas, government, and public utilities sectors. His experience varies across organization type and size, including government agencies.

He has taught at some leading institutions such as the Project Management Training Institute (Michigan, USA), The University of Calgary and Southern Alberta Institute of Technology. In Nigeria, he advises donor organizations like the British Department for International Development (DFID), the Nigeria Infrastructure Advisory Facility (NIAF), and state governments on the management and delivery of major infrastructure projects.

Tunde has worked extensively in the area of organizational management, project planning, audits, escalations, process reengineering, cost, and risk management along the entire value chain of significant capital projects in the construction, oil, and gas, and government sectors.

Tunde earned his B.Sc. (Hons) Degree in Civil Engineering from the University of Lagos (1984), MBA from Cranfield University School of Management, UK (1996), and Ph.D. from Department of Civil Engineering, University of Calgary, Canada (2011). His doctoral research focused on the dynamic complexities in large-scale public infrastructure projects.

Ayodele, Olayinka

Director

He started his career as a classroom teacher in 1982 and served across schools within the Ibeju Lekki Local Government Education Authority. He rose to the position of Head Teacher in 1992, a position he held till 2008. He afterward became and served as the Head of Section School support Services till 2017 and has been a Deputy Director of Education with the Lagos State Universal Basic Education Board since 2013.

A professional and diligent teacher, he holds a Grade II Teaching certificate from the Government Teacherâ€™s College, Epe, a Nigeria Certificate in Education from Ondo State College of Education in Ikere Ekiti and a Bachelor of Education in Management and Biology from the University of Port Harcourt.

He was a three times winner of the Ibeju Lekki Local Government Education Authority 1st Best Head Teacher Award in 1997, 2000& 2001, and a winner of the Lagos State Basic Education Board 3rd Best Teacher Award in 2008.

Mr. Ayodele has held several other positions from 2007 till date, including the Patron, National Youth Council of Nigeria Epe Chapter , Committee Chairman of LASCOFED Retreat at Peninsular Resort Ajah Lagos and LASCOFED Retreat at Equity Resort Ijebu-Ode Ogun State , Co-Team Leaders of (300) Three Hundred Cooperative Leaders to Lekki Free Zone, Tiye Ibeju-Lekki, Chairman Education Committee LASCOFED, Chairman Bookkeepers/Monitoring Committee LASCOFED and the 4TH Vice President/ Members of the Board of Director LASCOFED LTD.

Mr. Ayodele Olayinka was appointed a Director at Lekki Worldwide Investments Ltd in October 2013.

Amokomowo, Abiodun

Director

Amokomowo, Abiodun

Director

Abiodun Amokomowo was appointed Managing Director/CEO of IBILE Holdings Limited (the investment company of Lagos State Government) in September 2015, and he is responsible for the overall strategy and operations of the Company. Until his appointment, He was a Partner at Distinct Consult & Associates, a private consultancy firm in the area of Turnaround and Change Management; Operational Process Development and Management; Financial Planning and Investment Analysis; and Entrepreneurship and Leadership.

He began his career as an Audit Supervisor and later as a part-time Lecturer for Management Staff at Peat Marwick Ani Ogunde between July 1993 and August 1996, before venturing into the Banking Sector as an Assistant Manager, Credit, and Marketing at Crystal Bank from August 1996 till July 1997. He had a brief stint in the Public Sector when he served in a Treasury function at the Local Government Service Commission in Lagos between July 1997 and September 2000.

In September 2000, after contributing his quota to public service, he revived his banking career to perform a commercial banking function at Liberty Bank Plc till August 2001. After that, he moved to Omega bank as a unit head and later was appointed Acting Group Head, Consumer Banking at Omega Bank Plc till December 2003 before co-founding Distinct Consult & Associates in January 2004.

He had a short stint in the downstream sector of the telecommunication industry at the inception of the GSM business in Nigeria, with his organization being the biggest distributor of MTN and major distributor to other networks. On average, the company was doing a turnover of over N1 billion monthly with MTN alone.

During his career, Abiodun has been involved with investments in various activities in Nigeria, totaling more than N 500 billion.

Abiodun is a Chartered Accountant with over 25 years post ICAN qualification experience spanning across Audit, Banking Credit & Marketing, Treasury, Corporate/Commercial/Consumer Banking, Procurement, Logistics, and Consulting. He is a graduate of Economics (1993) and Accounting (1992) with MBA (2009). Abiodun qualified as a chartered accountant in November 1994 with the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria.
